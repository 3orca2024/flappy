🧍‍♂️ About Me

Hi! I'm Parker Champlin, a multi-sport athlete with a passion for adventure and competition. Whether I’m scaling cliffs as a dedicated rock climber, carving through powder as a skier, diving for spikes in volleyball, or swinging for the fences in softball, I thrive on pushing my limits and embracing the outdoors.

I’m currently a student at Dexter Southfield, where I balance academics with my athletic pursuits. Outside of school and sports, I’m a proud older sibling to my amazing fifth-grade sister, Scarlett, who keeps me grounded and inspired. Our family wouldn’t be complete without Orca, our energetic black-and-white dog who’s always up for a trail run or a snowy romp.

This site is a celebration of the sports I love, the people who support me, and the adventures that shape who I am. Dive into the games below and get a taste of the action!
